# OCD Exclusive Sweepstakes Partnership Agreement

**Document ID:** OCD-FS-001  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Status:** Draft — Attorney Review Required

This Agreement is entered into as of **[EFFECTIVE DATE]** by and among:

- **Obsessive Cannabis Disorder LLC** (“Brand Owner”);
- **[AUTHORIZED OPERATOR LEGAL NAME]** (“Authorized Operator”);
- **Duy Thai**, individually, solely for the limited Founder covenant expressly stated herein; and
- **Flora Summit LLC** (“Flora Summit”).

## 1. Partnership Purpose

The Parties desire to establish a coordinated long-term relationship in which Flora Summit serves as the exclusive partner for OCD-branded sweepstakes and related prize-promotion activities.

## 2. OCD Sweepstakes Activities

“OCD Sweepstakes Activities” means sweepstakes, promotional drawings, prize promotions, and substantially similar chance-based promotional programs conducted under, using, featuring, funded through, or materially associated with the OCD Brand or its goodwill.

The term does not include:

- ordinary coupons or fixed discounts;
- bona fide loyalty rewards with no element of chance;
- private employee incentives;
- charitable activities that do not use OCD goodwill commercially; or
- genuinely independent brands not constituting an OCD continuation.

## 3. Exclusive Appointment

Brand Owner appoints Flora Summit as the sole and exclusive commercial partner to develop, administer, operate, coordinate, or authorize OCD Sweepstakes Activities.

Authorized Operator acknowledges and agrees to honor the appointment.

No Party shall knowingly authorize another provider to perform OCD Sweepstakes Activities without Flora Summit's prior written consent.

## 4. Intended Duration

The Parties intend the appointment to be perpetual and brand-specific, surviving expiration or termination of campaign-level documents, Order Forms, Statements of Work, and ordinary service relationships.

The final execution version must include counsel-approved termination rights addressing at minimum:

- uncured material breach;
- illegality or regulatory prohibition;
- fraud or willful misconduct;
- insolvency;
- mutual written release; and
- any limits required for enforceability.

A campaign pause, lack of an active campaign, or change in service scope does not by itself terminate the appointment.

## 5. Authorized Operator Acknowledgment

Authorized Operator shall not directly or indirectly conduct, commission, outsource, sponsor, fund, license, or authorize OCD Sweepstakes Activities outside Flora Summit's appointment.

The appointment still applies when a processor, distributor, retailer, manufacturer, affiliate, prize supplier, or other participant is named as sponsor, advertiser, supplier, or contracting party.

## 6. Limited Founder Covenant

Duy Thai personally covenants that he will not knowingly use, direct, fund, cause, or participate in:

- a Controlled Entity;
- nominee;
- intermediary;
- undisclosed side agreement;
- under-the-table payment;
- referral arrangement;
- related operator; or
- another structure

for the principal purpose or material effect of moving OCD Sweepstakes Activities outside Flora Summit's appointment.

This covenant:

- is limited to OCD and its goodwill;
- does not guarantee performance by every separate entity;
- does not make Duy Thai personally liable for all company obligations;
- does not prohibit disclosed ordinary vendor or retailer payments; and
- does not cover genuinely independent brands.

## 7. Controlled Entities and Related Operators

Brand Owner and Authorized Operator shall not knowingly use a Controlled Entity, affiliate, successor, licensee, sublicensee, processor, distributor, management company, IP holding company, or related party to evade the appointment.

Before a controlled or replacement operator materially participates in OCD Sweepstakes Activities, Brand Owner shall cause it to execute an Operator Joinder where legally and commercially appropriate.

## 8. Change of Operator

A change in processor, distributor, manufacturer, manager, licensee, or operating company does not itself terminate Flora Summit's appointment.

Brand Owner shall provide the replacement operator written notice and use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain an acknowledgment before OCD Sweepstakes Activities begin.

## 9. Brand Evolution

The appointment applies to an OCD continuation, successor, replacement, or material evolution determined from the totality of circumstances, including transferred goodwill, customer migration, public positioning, reused principal assets, or a coordinated relaunch principally intended to avoid the appointment.

Common ownership alone does not make a genuinely separate brand subject to this Agreement.

## 10. Brand Transfer and Change of Control

Before transferring material OCD Brand Assets or control of Brand Owner, Brand Owner shall:

1. disclose Flora Summit's appointment to the proposed acquirer;
2. include it in relevant diligence and transaction schedules;
3. obtain a written assumption where required and legally available;
4. avoid representing that the assets are free of the appointment; and
5. give Flora Summit the contractually required notice.

Nothing purports to bind an unrelated buyer beyond applicable law or valid assumption. A failure by Brand Owner to follow this Section remains a breach.

## 11. Confidential or Concealed Arrangements

No Party shall knowingly conceal compensation, referral fees, side payments, nominee relationships, or indirect contracting structures principally intended to defeat Flora Summit's appointment.

This does not prohibit lawful, disclosed compensation paid in the ordinary course to retailers, vendors, processors, agencies, prize suppliers, or service providers.

## 12. Relationship to Flora Legal OS Agreements

This Agreement supplements the Flora Summit Master Commercial Agreement, applicable Order Forms, Statements of Work, and Sweepstakes Services Addendum.

For OCD Sweepstakes Activities, this Agreement controls over a conflicting general non-exclusivity clause, but only to the extent of the conflict.

Campaign economics, payment obligations, operational duties, and compliance responsibilities remain governed by the applicable Flora Summit commercial documents.

## 13. Compliance

Every OCD Sweepstakes Activity must be separately approved and conducted under applicable rules, disclosures, eligibility requirements, advertising restrictions, and regulatory requirements.

Exclusivity does not authorize unlawful conduct and does not guarantee that any proposed campaign will be approved or launched.

## 14. Remedies

The final version should provide:

- actual damages;
- equitable relief where legally available;
- specific performance where appropriate;
- recovery of fees and costs if approved;
- cure periods tailored to the breach; and
- no punitive liquidated amount that functions as an unenforceable penalty.

## 15. General Terms

Final counsel-approved terms must address notices, amendments, waiver, integration, assignment, successors, severability, governing law, venue, dispute resolution, electronic signatures, counterparts, attorneys' fees, and survival.

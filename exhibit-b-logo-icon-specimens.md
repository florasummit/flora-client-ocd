# OCD Exclusive Sweepstakes Partnership Agreement

**Document ID:** OCD-AGR-002  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Status:** Draft — Attorney Review Required  

This Agreement is entered into by and among:

- **Obsessive Cannabis Disorder LLC**, as Brand Owner;
- **[AUTHORIZED OPERATOR LEGAL NAME]**, as Authorized Operator;
- **Duy Thai**, individually, solely for the Founder acknowledgment and covenant expressly stated herein; and
- **Flora Summit LLC**, as Exclusive Sweepstakes Partner.

## 1. Commercial Purpose

The Parties desire to establish a coordinated long-term relationship under which Flora Summit LLC serves as the exclusive partner for OCD-branded sweepstakes and related prize-promotion activities.

## 2. Exclusive Appointment

Brand Owner appoints Flora Summit LLC as the sole and exclusive provider, administrator, marketing partner, and authorized commercial operator for OCD Sweepstakes Activities.

“OCD Sweepstakes Activities” means sweepstakes, promotional drawings, prize promotions, and substantially similar chance-based promotional programs conducted under, using, featuring, or materially associated with the OCD brand or its goodwill.

## 3. Duration and Survival

The Parties intend the exclusive appointment to be perpetual and to survive expiration or termination of campaign-level documents, Order Forms, Statements of Work, and service relationships, except as expressly modified or released in a later written agreement signed by Brand Owner and Flora Summit LLC.

Counsel must confirm the final termination, material-breach, insolvency, illegality, and enforceability provisions before execution.

## 4. Authorized Operator Acknowledgment

Authorized Operator acknowledges the exclusive appointment and agrees that it will not independently conduct, commission, outsource, license, sponsor, or authorize OCD Sweepstakes Activities except through Flora Summit LLC or with Flora Summit LLC's prior written consent.

A campaign does not fall outside this Agreement merely because Authorized Operator, a retailer, processor, distributor, manufacturer, affiliate, or another permitted participant is identified as sponsor, prize contributor, product supplier, advertiser, or contracting party.

## 5. Founder Personal Covenant

Duy Thai personally covenants that he will not knowingly use, direct, fund, or cause an entity he controls, a nominee, intermediary, undisclosed side arrangement, or a controlled related party to conduct OCD Sweepstakes Activities for the principal purpose or material effect of avoiding Flora Summit LLC's exclusive appointment.

This is a limited OCD-specific covenant. It does not make Duy Thai personally responsible for all company debts or apply to genuinely independent brands that do not constitute a continuation or exploitation of OCD goodwill.

## 6. Related and Controlled Entities

Brand Owner and Authorized Operator shall not use a present or future affiliate, successor, licensee, sublicensee, processor, distributor, management company, intellectual-property holding company, or entity under common control to evade the exclusive appointment.

Where a controlled entity is expected to materially exploit the OCD brand in connection with OCD Sweepstakes Activities, the applicable Party shall cause that entity to sign a commercially reasonable acknowledgment or joinder before the activity begins.

## 7. Change of Operator

Changing the processor, distributor, manufacturer, licensee, manager, or operating entity associated with OCD does not itself terminate Flora Summit LLC's appointment.

Brand Owner shall provide written notice of the appointment to each replacement operator and obtain an acknowledgment where commercially and legally appropriate.

## 8. Brand Transfer and Change of Control

Before a transfer of material OCD Brand Assets or control of Brand Owner, Brand Owner shall:

1. disclose Flora Summit LLC's appointment to the proposed acquirer;
2. include the appointment in relevant transaction schedules;
3. obtain a written assumption when required by the final transfer documents; and
4. avoid representing that the OCD Brand Assets are free of the disclosed appointment.

## 9. No Concealed Workaround

Brand Owner, Authorized Operator, and Duy Thai shall not knowingly conceal compensation, side payments, referral arrangements, nominee relationships, or indirect contracting structures principally intended to cause OCD Sweepstakes Activities to be performed outside Flora Summit LLC's appointment.

Ordinary disclosed payments to retailers, vendors, processors, agencies, prize suppliers, or service providers are not prohibited merely because they involve third parties.

## 10. Independent Brands

This Agreement does not automatically apply to every brand owned by Duy Thai, Brand Owner, Authorized Operator, or their affiliates. It applies to OCD and any brand shown by the totality of circumstances to be a continuation, successor, replacement, or material evolution of OCD or its goodwill.

## 11. Relationship to Flora Legal OS Agreements

This Agreement supplements the Flora Summit Master Commercial Agreement and Sweepstakes Services Addendum maintained in `flora-legal-os`.

For OCD Sweepstakes Activities, this Agreement controls over a conflicting general non-exclusivity provision, but only to the extent of the conflict.

## 12. Remedies and Enforcement

The final execution version should provide commercially reasonable remedies, including damages and equitable relief where legally available, while avoiding unenforceable penalties or restraints broader than the OCD-specific legitimate business interests documented here.

## 13. General Terms

The final execution version must include approved provisions addressing governing law, venue, notices, amendments, assignment, successors, counterparts, electronic signatures, severability, waiver, integration, attorneys' fees, and survival.

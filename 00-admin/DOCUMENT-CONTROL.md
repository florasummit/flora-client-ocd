# Document Control Policy

1. Every document must display a Document ID, version, and status.
2. Drafts may be changed only in source Markdown.
3. Generated files may be replaced by later builds.
4. Executed files in `10-signed/` may never be overwritten.
5. Amendments, joinders, assumptions, notices, and releases must be separate dated documents.
6. Store signature completion certificates in `08-records/signatures/`.
7. Store a SHA-256 manifest in `08-records/hashes/`.
8. Do not store passwords, private keys, full Social Security numbers, payment-card data, or unnecessary identity documents in GitHub.
9. Any factual uncertainty must remain marked `REQUIRED` or be disclosed—not guessed.

## Final Business Structure Decisions (July 2026)

- Obsessive Cannabis Disorder LLC is and remains the sole owner of the OCD Brand, trademarks, goodwill, and related intellectual property unless expressly assigned in writing.
- Flora Summit LLC is an independent marketing, advertising, promotions, sweepstakes, compliance, and technology services company.
- The relationship is an exclusive contractual partnership and is not intended to transfer ownership of the OCD Brand or create a joint venture.
- Flora's exclusive rights apply to current and future OCD brands, worldwide expansion, successors, permitted assigns, and transfers of the brand, subject to applicable law.
- OCD may not unilaterally terminate Flora's exclusive rights. Any material breach by Flora requires written notice and a minimum 120-day cure period (or longer while diligently curing if reasonably incapable of completion within that period), except where prohibited by applicable law.

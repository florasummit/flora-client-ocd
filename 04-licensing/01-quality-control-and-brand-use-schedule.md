# Quality-Control and Brand-Use Schedule

**Document ID:** OCD-LIC-002  
**Status:** Draft

Authorized Operator shall:

1. use only approved OCD marks and current artwork;
2. comply with applicable packaging, labeling, advertising, and licensing requirements;
3. avoid modifying core marks without Brand Owner approval;
4. maintain reasonable product and packaging consistency;
5. promptly report recalls, material complaints, infringement claims, or regulatory action affecting the brand;
6. avoid representing itself as owner of the OCD Brand;
7. stop using superseded or unauthorized artwork when reasonably directed; and
8. provide representative specimens upon reasonable request.

Brand Owner's quality control concerns brand integrity and does not authorize Brand Owner to direct regulated operations in a manner inconsistent with applicable law.

# Founder Brand Ownership Declaration

**Document ID:** OCD-OWN-001  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Status:** Draft — Attorney Review Required

I, **Duy Thai**, declare as follows:

## 1. Purpose

This Declaration records my good-faith knowledge concerning the origin, adoption, ownership, development, and commercial use of the OCD / Obsessive Cannabis Disorder brand before the contemplated transfer or confirmation of ownership in Obsessive Cannabis Disorder LLC.

## 2. Statements

After reasonable inquiry, I state that:

1. I created, selected, adopted, commissioned, acquired, or otherwise developed the OCD brand and the assets identified in the attached schedules, except as specifically disclosed.
2. I have disclosed every person or entity known to me that may claim authorship, ownership, licensing, royalty, security, approval, partnership, profit participation, or other material rights involving the brand.
3. I have disclosed all written and oral agreements materially affecting the brand.
4. I have not knowingly transferred the same rights to another person except as disclosed.
5. No undisclosed agreement known to me prohibits the contemplated assignment or licensing.
6. I will promptly disclose any later-discovered ownership claim, restriction, lien, dispute, or third-party interest.

## 3. Limited Personal Nature

I sign personally because the historical chain of title depends on the accuracy of my disclosures. This Declaration does not, by itself, make me personally liable for every obligation or debt of any limited liability company.

## 4. Regulatory Separation

This Declaration concerns brand and intellectual-property rights only. It does not transfer or establish ownership of an OMMA license, licensed cannabis business, regulated inventory, or licensed premises.
